Meatballs

Description

This recipe constains meatballs and spagghtti which is vital for the growing body

I will provide you the means on how to make a meatball and spagheti meatball

Ingredients

  • Meatballs
  • Ririoni spaghetti
  • Buffalo sauce
  • Condiment of your choosing

Instructions

  1. Put all ingredients in a bowl
  2. dour it in oil
  3. put in freezer for two hours
  4. take it out and put max heat in oven for 30 min
  5. take out and enjoy
